Welcome to Making Waves - the hub for marine life enthusiasts! Our organization was founded to tackle the growing concerns around climate change and its impact on the marine population. We strive to create greater awareness about the importance of conserving marine life, and work towards finding sustainable solutions to reduce contamination and water pollution.


We're proud to announce that we're currently working on creating curriculum and video resources to help spread awareness about sustainability and reducing marine contamination. We believe that education is the key to making a real difference, and we're excited to share our knowledge with you.


Join us on our social media accounts - Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram - to stay up-to-date with the latest marine life news and events. Follow us as we make waves and work towards a healthier planet!
